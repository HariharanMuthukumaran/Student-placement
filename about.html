In Detail

In modern days,securing placements during graduate studies has become challenging so the strategic analysis of student performance plays a pivotal role in forecasting their potential selections for placements in any University or an affiliated institutions. The primary goal of higher education institutions is to provide quality education for better placement opportunities to the match the need of recruitment process. This system can be used by a placement officer in the higher education institution to manage student information. The value of this approach extends beyond prediction by analyzing student performance. This can predict student selection for placements. This algorithm helps to identify student’s eligible for various companies. Through a systematic analysis of student performance, this approach facilitates the prediction of suitable candidates for various placements by analyzing the student’s CGPA, skill score, and history of arrears. Moreover, this system equips the placement officers with a powerful tool for efficient student management allowing them to track progress, to address challenges, and to enhance overall placement success rates.
